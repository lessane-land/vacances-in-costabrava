05 / Itinerary

Day by day

Day 01
Thu · Oct 1

Madrid → Girona → L'Escala

AVE to Girona, pick up the car at the station and head to L'Escala (40 min). Easy check-in, evening walk by the port and dinner at a seaside spot with Mediterranean views.

🚄 Train🚗 Car🍷 Dinner
Day 02
Fri · Oct 2

L'Escala + Empúries on foot

Morning exploring the town and coastal walk to the Roman and Greek ruins of Empúries (on foot from the apartment). One of the great archaeological gems of the Mediterranean.

🏛 Culture🥾 Hiking🌊 Beach
Day 03
Sat · Oct 3

Cadaqués + Portlligat

Drive to Cadaqués (50 min). Visit Salvador Dalí's House-Museum in Portlligat — book online well in advance, very limited capacity. Afternoon of coves and vermouth.

🎨 Dalí🏖 Coves📍 Book ahead
Day 04
Sun · Oct 4

Figueres · Dalí Theatre-Museum

35 min drive to Figueres for the most surprising and surreal museum in Spain. Lunch of Empordà cuisine: botifarra, pa amb tomàquet and a good red wine.

🎨 Museum🍽 Food
Day 05
Mon · Oct 5

Camí de Ronda · Roses → Cap de Creus

35 min to Roses to start one of the most spectacular stretches of the GR-92 trail: cliffs, hidden coves, turquoise water. Bring proper shoes, water and a sandwich.

🥾 Trekking🌊 GR-92
Day 06
Tue · Oct 6

Cap de Creus Natural Park + Roses

Lunar landscapes, wind, rocks sculpted by the sea. Cap de Creus lighthouse at sunset (iconic) and a final swim in Roses if the weather plays along.

🌋 Nature🌅 Sunset
Day 07
Wed · Oct 7

L'Escala slow · anchovies & market

A car-free day. Local market, tasting of the famous L'Escala anchovies, beach walk and farewell dinner at one of the port restaurants.

🐟 Anchovies😌 Slow day
Day 08
Thu · Oct 8

Back to Madrid

Check-out, drive to Girona (40 min), return the car and train home. Goodbye, Costa Brava — until next time.

🚄 Train↩ Return

09 / Flavors

Must-try cuisine

L'Escala
World-famous salted anchovies, fresh seafood, rice dishes.
Cadaqués
Casa Anita (traditional, no reservations) · Compartir (creative fine dining).
Roses
Fresh fish restaurants along the harbor, perfect at lunch.
Figueres
Empordà cuisine: botifarra, pa amb tomàquet, escalivada, DO Empordà wines.
10 / Hiking

Camí de Ronda GR-92

Cap de Creus
Lunar landscape, wild coves, dramatic cliffs. Protected Natural Park.
Roses → Cadaqués
The most spectacular stretch: open sea views, ~4h one way.
L'Escala → Empúries
Easy coastal walk, mix of sea and archaeology. Perfect to start.
October
Best month: mild temperatures, few crowds, golden Mediterranean autumn light.
