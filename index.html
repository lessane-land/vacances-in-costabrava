Tramuntana Wind Warning

The Alt Empordà is famous for the tramuntana — a cold, powerful north wind up to 100 km/h. It can arrive suddenly, especially autumn and winter. Beautiful and brutal.

Best seasons
★ BEST
Spring (Apr–May)
Perfect 16–22°C. Wildflowers on Cap de Creus. Green fields. Best light for the monastery.
★ BEST
Autumn (Sep–Oct)
Golden light, harvest season. 18–24°C. Tramuntana risk grows in October. Ideal overall.
Summer (Jun–Aug)
Very hot 28–35°C. Start at 6am. Extra water. Dalí Museum packed.
Winter (Nov–Mar)
Cold 5–12°C. Strong tramuntana likely. Some services closed. Beautiful solitude.
